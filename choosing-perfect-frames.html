Choosing the Perfect Frames for Your Face Shape

Posted on May 31, 2023

Finding the perfect frames for your face shape is crucial to enhance your appearance and style. The right eyewear can complement your features and highlight your best attributes. Whether you have a round, oval, square, heart-shaped, or diamond-shaped face, we have expert tips to help you choose frames that suit your unique face shape.

Frames for Round Faces

If you have a round face, angular frames can add definition and balance. Look for frames with sharp edges, such as rectangular or square shapes. Avoid round or overly curved frames that can make your face appear rounder.

Frames for Oval Faces

Oval faces are versatile and can pull off various frame styles. You can experiment with different shapes, including rectangular, round, or cat-eye frames. Focus on proportion and choose frames that are slightly wider than the widest part of your face.

Frames for Square Faces

Softening the angles of a square face can be achieved with round or oval frames. Look for frames with curved edges to contrast the strong features of your face. Avoid square or geometric frames that may accentuate your face's angularity.

Frames for Heart-Shaped Faces

Heart-shaped faces are characterized by a wider forehead and narrower chin. To balance your face, opt for frames that are wider at the bottom, such as cat-eye or round frames. Rimless or light-colored frames can also work well for this face shape.

Frames for Diamond-Shaped Faces

Diamond-shaped faces have broad cheekbones and a narrower forehead and chin. Choose frames that accentuate your eyes and highlight your cheekbones. Cat-eye or oval frames can be flattering options for this face shape.
