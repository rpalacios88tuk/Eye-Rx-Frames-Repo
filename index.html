Patients Own Frame Policy

At Eye Rx Frames, we understand that some patients may prefer to use their own frames when ordering prescription lenses. That's why we offer a Patients Own Frame service for those who want to keep their existing frames or purchase new frames from a different source.

When you choose our Patients Own Frame service, you can rest assured that your frames will be handled with the utmost care and expertise by our trained technicians. We will take precise measurements of your frames to ensure a perfect fit for your lenses, and we use only the highest-quality materials and advanced technology to create your lenses.

Please note that while we take every precaution to ensure the safe handling of your frames, we cannot be held responsible for any damage that may occur during the lens installation process. Additionally, if your frames are found to be unsuitable for lens installation, we will inform you before proceeding with the service and may offer alternative options.

To take advantage of our Patients Own Frame service, simply bring your frames to one of our locations or send them to us with your prescription information. Our team will take care of the rest and return your frames to you with your new lenses installed.

Thank you for choosing Eye Rx Frames for your eyewear needs. We are committed to providing you with the best possible products and services, and our Patients Own Frame service is just one example of our dedication to meeting our patients' individual needs.

Lens Redo Policy

  1. Eye Rx Frames will redo lenses for customers within 60 days of the original purchase date, provided that the customer meets the criteria outlined below.
  2. To qualify for a redo, the customer must provide a valid proof of purchase and return the original eyeglasses or lenses in their original condition.
  3. The customer must schedule an appointment with an optician or eyecare professional at Eye Rx Frames to determine the reason for the dissatisfaction and to discuss the desired changes.
  4. If the dissatisfaction is due to a prescription change, the customer must provide a new prescription from their eye doctor before the redo can be performed.
  5. If the dissatisfaction is due to the lens material or coating, the customer must pay the difference in cost between the original and desired lenses.
  6. If the dissatisfaction is due to a mistake or error made by Eye Rx Frames, the redo will be performed at no additional charge to the customer.
  7. Eye Rx Frames reserves the right to deny a redo if the customer has damaged or altered the original lenses or frames in any way.
  8. Eye Rx Frames is not responsible for any loss or damage to the original lenses or frames during the redo process.
  9. The customer acknowledges that a redo may result in slight differences in the appearance, fit, or function of the eyewear, and agrees to accept such changes as reasonable and acceptable.
  10. This lens redo policy is subject to change at any time without prior notice to customers.

Anti-Glare Warranty Policy

At Eye Rx Frames, we are committed to providing our customers with high-quality eyeglasses and sunglasses that meet their needs and exceed their expectations. That's why we offer an Anti-Glare Warranty for all of our lenses with anti-glare coating.

This warranty covers any defects in the anti-glare coating for a period of one year from the date of purchase. If the anti-glare coating on your lenses becomes defective during this time, we will replace your lenses free of charge.

Please note that this warranty does not cover damage caused by accidents, misuse, or improper care of your eyeglasses or sunglasses. Additionally, this warranty does not cover scratches or other cosmetic damage to the lenses.
